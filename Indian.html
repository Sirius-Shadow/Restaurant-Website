Our Indian Selection

Immerse yourself in the authentic flavors of India, where each dish celebrates tradition, spices, and cultural diversity. Our carefully curated menu brings you the finest Indian dishes, prepared with fresh ingredients and time-honored cooking methods.

Butter Chicken

A creamy and mildly spiced curry, combining tender pieces of marinated chicken with a rich, tomato-based sauce, flavored with a blend of aromatic spices.

Biryani

A fragrant rice dish, layered with marinated meat or vegetables, and cooked with a unique blend of spices, creating a symphony of flavors.

Samosas

Crispy, golden pastries filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat, providing a deliciously savory treat that’s perfect for any occasion.

Cultural Heritage

In Indian culture, food is a celebration of spices, flavors, and the joy of sharing. Our dishes honor these customs while bringing you authentic flavors from various regions of India.

Dining Customs - Thali

The "Thali" is a traditional way of serving a variety of dishes on a single platter, symbolizing the diversity and harmony of Indian flavors. Our restaurant recreates this experience, allowing you to enjoy a range of tastes and textures in one meal.
