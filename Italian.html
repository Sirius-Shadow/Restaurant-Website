Our Italian Selection

Immerse yourself in the authentic flavors of Italy, where every dish celebrates tradition, fresh ingredients, and cultural heritage. Our carefully curated menu brings you the finest Italian dishes, prepared with fresh local ingredients and time-honored cooking methods.

Traditional Neapolitan Pizza

A classic Italian masterpiece featuring hand-stretched dough topped with San Marzano tomatoes, fresh buffalo mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves, baked to perfection in our wood-fired oven.

Homemade Pasta

Expertly crafted pasta dishes including rich Spaghetti Bolognese with slow-cooked ragù and creamy Fettuccine Alfredo, made fresh daily in our kitchen.

Tiramisu

Our signature dessert featuring delicate layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, dusted with premium cocoa powder. A true taste of Italian indulgence.
