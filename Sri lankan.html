Experience Sri Lankan Cuisine

Discover the vibrant flavors and rich culinary heritage of Sri Lanka

Our Sri Lankan Selection

Immerse yourself in the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka, where every dish tells a story of tradition, spice, and cultural heritage. Our carefully curated menu brings you the finest Sri Lankan dishes, prepared with authentic spices and traditional cooking methods.

Rice and Curry

A classic Sri Lankan meal featuring fragrant rice served with an assortment of flavorful curries. Choose from chicken, fish, or vegetable varieties, each prepared with our signature blend of spices.

Hoppers (Appa)

Crispy, bowl-shaped rice flour pancakes with perfectly cooked eggs. A beloved Sri Lankan breakfast dish that's equally delightful for dinner.

Kottu Roti

A street food favorite featuring chopped roti stir-fried with fresh vegetables, your choice of meat, and aromatic spices. A true taste of Sri Lankan street cuisine.

Cultural Heritage

In Sri Lankan culture, meals are more than just sustenance - they're a celebration of community and tradition. Our dishes honor these customs while bringing you authentic flavors from the island nation.

Dining Customs

Traditional Sri Lankan meals are often served on banana leaves and eaten with the right hand, though we provide modern table settings while maintaining the authentic flavors and presentation of each dish.
